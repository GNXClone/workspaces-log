Symptom

  • Status indicator stayed yellow after launchd relaunched PittsBot.
  • UI lied about process health — effectively frozen on the first PID observed.

Root cause

  • launchd_status.current() cached the first-observed parent PID via lru_cache.
  • After relaunch the parent PID changed; the cached reader never re-read os.getppid().
  • So the indicator compared against a dead ancestor and refused to flip.

Fix

  • Dropped the cache.
  • current() now re-reads os.getppid() live on every call.
  • Replaced cached-semantics test with a re-read test that flips ppid mid-run and verifies the status tracks the change.

Why it’s fine to do this

  • os.getppid() is a syscall, nanoseconds. The cache was never load-bearing.
  • Silent-staleness bugs are the worst kind; re-reading is pure improvement.
